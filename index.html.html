Online Education

Online method of education can be a highly effective method of education for the students who are matures, self-disciplined and motivated, well organized and having high degree of time management skills but it is an inappropriate learning environment for more dependent learners and has difficulty assuming responsibilities requirred by the online education.


So here my motivation factors towards online education:

1. Always think about my parents.

I lost my dad to cancer 4 years ago, the last words that he said to me is belajar rajin-rajin. He wants me to study hard. Since that day, I made that words as my passion for me to study hard. I don't want to disappoint my dad

2. Ask for reward.

I asked my brother to buy me some delicious foods after I finishing each assingment that has been given. Well, its kinda force my brother actually. But, it help me to feel motivate after I got my reward.

3. Use positive self-talk.

Tell myself This isn't so hard, it won't take so long and i am sure that I know how to do it, or that I can learn while I'm doing it. By doing this, its telling myself that what I do is good enough makes the tasks looks small and easy in my mind.
